Privacy Policy — Drivemate App

Last updated: 06 April 2026

1. Introduction

Drivemate (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Drivemate mobile application.

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. This Privacy Policy is provided in accordance with Google Play Developer Policies and applicable data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number, profile photo
  • Student information (course details, progress, payments)
  • Vehicle information (registration, insurance)
  • Driver’s license and endorsements
  • Payment and transaction records

2.2 Device & Technical Information

  • Device type, operating system, app version
  • IP address, browser type
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

2.3 Usage Data

  • App interactions and feature usage
  • Session duration and activity logs

2.4 Sensitive Data (Requires Explicit Consent)

Location Data

  • Precise location
  • Driving routes and movement patterns
  • Background location during active lessons

Camera Data

  • Photos of documents (license, ID, vehicle registration)
  • Profile images

Contacts (Optional Feature)

  • Names and phone numbers for invitation purposes
  • Accessed temporarily and not stored

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate driving school management services
  • Track driving lessons and generate route history
  • Monitor driver performance and ensure student safety
  • Process payments and maintain records
  • Send service updates and notifications
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Detect fraud and enhance security
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Prominent Permissions Disclosure (Google Play Requirement)

Location Permission

  • Track driving lesson routes
  • Calculate distance traveled
  • Monitor driving performance
  • Ensure safety during lessons

Background Location Usage: Location may be collected even when the app is not in use, but only during active lesson sessions.

Camera Permission

  • Capture identity and vehicle documents
  • Take profile photos

Camera is only accessed when initiated by the user.

Contacts Permission (Optional)

  • Invite contacts via WhatsApp

Contacts are accessed only with your permission and are not stored.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data.

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, support)

5.1 Location Data Sharing

  • Driving instructors and administrators (live tracking)

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Firebase Firestore

Security measures include data encryption, access controls, and regular updates. However, no system is completely secure.
