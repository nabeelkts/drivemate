## 📋 Google Play Console Setup Steps

### Step 1: Create New App in Play Console

1. Go to [Google Play Console](https://play.google.com/console)
2. Click **"Create app"**
3. Fill in:
   - **App name**: Drivemate
   - **Default language**: English (United States)
   - **App or game**: App
   - **Free or paid**: Free (or Paid based on your model)
4. Accept **Developer Policy Agreement**
5. Click **"Create app"**

---

### Step 2: Set Up App Information

#### **Main Store Listing**
1. Navigate to **Main store listing**
2. Fill in:
   - **App name**: Drivemate
   - **Short description**: (80 chars max) e.g., "Your one-stop driving service management app"
   - **Full description**: (4000 chars max) Detailed description of features
   - **App icon**: 512x512 PNG (your existing logo)
   - **Feature graphic**: 1024x500 PNG
   - **Screenshots**: At least 2 screenshots (phone & tablet)
   - **Category**: Choose appropriate category (e.g., Productivity, Business)
   - **Contact email**: Your support email
   - **Contact phone**: Optional
   - **Contact website**: Optional

#### **Graphic Assets**
- **App icon**: Use `assets/icons/logo.png` (ensure it's 512x512)
- **Screenshots**: Take screenshots of your app's main screens
- **Feature graphic**: Create a 1024x500 promotional banner

---

### Step 3: Content Rating

1. Go to **App content** → **Content rating**
2. Click **"Get started"**
3. Complete the IARC questionnaire
4. Submit and receive your age rating

---

### Step 4: Privacy Policy

1. Go to **App content** → **Privacy policy**
2. Click **"Get started"**
3. Provide a URL to your privacy policy
4. If you don't have one, create a privacy policy page on your website

**Important**: Your app collects:
- Location data (GPS)
- User information (name, email, phone)
- Document images (licenses, vehicle info)
- Contact information

Make sure your privacy policy covers all these data types!

---

### Step 5: Ads Declaration

1. Go to **App content** → **Ads**
2. Declare whether your app contains ads or not
3. Select **"No"** if your app doesn't show ads

---

1. Go to **Data safety** section

## 🚀 Build and Upload Release

### Build Release Bundle

```bash

```

Output file: `build/app/outputs/bundle/release/app-release.aab`

### Upload to Play Console

1. Go to **Production** (or **Open testing** / **Internal testing**)
2. Click **"Create new release"**
3. Upload your `.aab` file
4. Add release notes:

5. Click **"Next"** and follow the prompts
6. Review and click **"Start rollout to Production"**

## 🔐 Keystore Security Reminder

**CRITICAL**: Keep your keystore safe!

✅ **DO**:
- Backup your keystore file to multiple secure locations
- Remember your passwords (store password & key password)
- Use the same keystore for all future updates

❌ **DON'T**:
- Never commit keystore to Git
- Never share your keystore passwords
- Never lose your keystore (can't update app without it!)

## 📊 Release Tracks Explained

### Internal Testing (Recommended First)
- Up to 100 testers
- Fastest review time (~minutes)
- Good for initial testing

### Closed Testing
- Specific email lists or Google Groups
- Review time: ~1-2 days
- Good for beta testing

### Open Testing
- Anyone can join via link
- Review time: ~2-3 days
- Good for public beta

### Production
- Live on Play Store
- Review time: ~3-7 days (first release), ~1-2 days (updates)
- Available to all users

**Recommended Strategy**: 
1. Start with **Internal Testing** with your team
2. Move to **Closed Testing** with trusted users
3. Then **Open Testing** for broader feedback
4. Finally **Production** rollout

## ⚠️ Common Issues and Solutions

### Issue: "App bundle is signed in debug mode"
**Solution**: ✅ Already fixed! You're now using release signing.

### Issue: "Package name is restricted"
**Solution**: ✅ Already fixed! Changed from `com.example.mds` to `com.drivemate.mds`.

### Issue: "You need to register the app signature"
**Solution**: 
1. Get your SHA-1 fingerprint:
   ```bash
   keytool -list -v -keystore C:\Users\YourUsername\upload-keystore.jks -alias upload
   ```
2. Add SHA-1 to Firebase Console:
   - Go to Firebase Console → Project Settings
   - Add SHA-1 certificate fingerprint
   - Download updated `google-services.json`

### Issue: "Missing permissions declaration"
**Solution**: 
- Go to **App content** → **Permissions**
- Declare each permission your app uses
- Explain why each permission is needed
